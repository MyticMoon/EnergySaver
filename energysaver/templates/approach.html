Our mission: From the bottom of our hearts, we want to build a community that promotes and values electricity saving as well as personal carbon footprints reduction. Because, what's the point of killing trees paying for electricity if you are not using it ?

1

Stop wasting

Every kWh of electricity emits 0.97kg of CO2. That is, by leaving your water heater on unnecessarily for an hour, you emit 27kg of CO2 into the atmosphere.

By wasting electricity, not only are you 'burning' cash, you are also diminishing the vitality of our Mother Earth. So stop wasting...
